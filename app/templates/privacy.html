Privacy Policy

ClassFinder is a web application that allows users to track their classes. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data.

Information we collect

With an account

When you create an account, we collect the following information:

  • School email
  • Username
  • Classes

Without an account

If you use ClassFinder without creating an account, we collect the pages you have been on, and how long it has taken to load them.

This information is stored anonymously and is used to improve our service.

Analytics

Analytics are used to get the total number of requests to the site, and are never linked to you.

How we use your information

Your information is never sold and is only used to provide you with personalized class information.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, or would like us to delete analytics data, please email us at help@trey7658.com.
