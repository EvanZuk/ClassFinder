Privacy Policy

Alot of information is not complete, while this site is in beta, assume all data is collected

This is the privacy policy for ClassFinder. It outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Only data you willingly add to the site will be collected. All data can be removed by visiting this link. This includes data such as username, full name, and linked classes.

Class information cannot be deleted, only the link between you and the class.

While class information may not be able to be deleted with your account, all class data is removed at the end of a semester.

While your account is active, your connection to a class may be public, this is intended for people with the same class to find others with that class. You should assume that all class data is public.

How We Use Your Information

Data is only used for website functions, like showing current class.

Information Sharing

No data is shared with third parties, except in the case of law enforcement.

Security Measures

Data such as passwords are stored with hashing algorithms, and raw passwords are never saved.

Cookies

Cookies are not able to be disabled as they are for basic website function.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may change at any time without notice. Last updated on Aug 29 2024
